1 Revenue Achieved & Net Cost

Source: BOXMANAGER. These columns are populated directly from BOXMANAGER, based on entries made by the respective CSE. They reflect the data as it stands in the system at the time of refresh.

2 Revenue Attainment %

Calculated from your target against the revenue achieved as recorded in BOXMANAGER.

Revenue Attainment % = Revenue Achieved ÷ Revenue Goal × 100

3 Revenue Gap to Goal & GM Gap to Goal

These are your primary indicators. They show your current position against the goal — how much is left to achieve (shown in red), or, where the value is negative, how much you have already exceeded the goal by (shown in green).

4 GM Achieved For reference

Based purely on CSE entries and shown for your reference. This amount is subject to approval from Management following the quarterly P&L evaluation.

5 Collected Gross Margin For reference

The portion of gross margin tied to amounts actually collected against invoices.

Collected GM = Total Quarterly GM × ( Amount Collected ÷ Total Invoiced Value )
