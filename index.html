1 Revenue Goal Goal Letter

Your quarterly revenue target, provided to you via Zoho People in your personal Goal Letter. The Q2 goal remains unchanged; your Q3 goal will be issued by the end of June.

2 Invoiced Revenue

Source: Zoho Books. The value of actual invoices sent to your customers during the quarter. It reflects the data as it stands in Zoho Books at the time of refresh.

3 Revenue Attainment %

Your invoiced revenue measured against your revenue goal.

Revenue Attainment % = Invoiced Revenue ÷ Revenue Goal × 100

4 Core GM Achieved For reference

Source: BOXMANAGER / TMS. Operational gross margin based on CSE entries. Per policy, Core GM is shown for visibility only and is not the incentive metric. It is subject to Management approval after the quarterly P&L evaluation.

5 Payment Collected

Source: Zoho Books. Eligible payments received against invoices during the quarter. Excludes VAT, duties and taxes, customs recoveries, non-sales recoveries and zero-margin reimbursements; credit notes, write-offs and reversals are deducted, and disputed invoices are excluded until resolved.

6 Collected GM Goal

The Collected Gross Margin target for your portfolio for the quarter — the benchmark the Collected GM Achieved and Gap to Target columns are measured against. Previously 'GM Target' is replaced and referred as 'Collected GM Goal'.

7 Collected GM Achieved Primary incentive metric

This is the metric incentives are based on. On this dashboard it is derived from your eligible collections and your previous-quarter GM%:

Collected GM Achieved = Payment Collected × Previous Quarter GM%

The figure shown here is indicative. The binding definition of Collected GM for incentive calculation is set out in the policy, where it is computed from eligible collections and the applicable GM%, then validated by Finance against Zoho Books and BOXMANAGER / TMS before any payout.

8 Collected GM Attainment %

Your Collected GM Achieved measured against your Collected GM Goal. This drives the colour of the Gap to Target column.

Collected GM Attainment % = Collected GM Achieved ÷ Collected GM Goal × 100

9 Collected GM Gap to Target Primary indicator

Your headline position against the goal — how much Collected GM is left to realise (red), or how far you have already exceeded the goal (green).

Gap to Target = Collected GM Goal − Collected GM Achieved

i How incentives are determined

Incentives are calculated on Collected GM only. Eligibility and the payout percentage are governed by the policy:

• Eligibility is based on the previous quarter GM% threshold (below 12% — not eligible; 12–14.99% — 50%; 15–19.99% — 75%; 20–24.99% — 100%; 25%+ — 120%). HRP-04 §8

• Payout rate on Collected GM is 5% for CDM / NDM and 3% for CSE / Support. HRP-04 §9

• The quarterly payout is capped at 1× monthly fixed salary. HRP-04 §10

• All payouts are subject to Finance validation and Management approval. HRP-04 §14 HRP-04 §15

⚠️

Incentive note

Incentives are payable only on Collected Gross Margin. All Core GM and Collected GM figures above are provided for your reference and are indicative until the quarterly P&L evaluation is finalised.

The final incentive payout is subject to Management approval after the P&L evaluation, and may be adjusted where there is a material variance between operational GP (BOXMANAGER / TMS) and accounting records (Zoho Books).

§ Where the rules come from

All definitions, thresholds and payout conditions on this report follow the Sales Compensation & Incentive Policy — HRP‑04 (Rev 00 · Issue 03). If any figure or rule is unclear, refer to that document (latest issue, available from Finance / HR or your line manager) before raising a query.
